About Us

We are a student-led publication reimagining the role of youth in business, economics, and finance. Not as silent spectators, but as informed, intentional architects of the future. Born out of both ambition and absence: the absence of access, the absence of youth voices, the absence of financial clarity, this magazine was founded to challenge the status quo. In a world where economic systems are often gatekept and financial literacy remains a privilege, we are here to change the narrative. Through youth-driven articles, commentary, interviews, and creative thought, we decode the complex, spotlight the overlooked, and confront the inequities that shape markets and minds alike. Our mission is simple but urgent: to equip our generation with the tools to not just understand finance, but to question it, shape it, and lead it with conscience. We do not believe that where you come from should define how far you go. Founded by female Pakistani students, this platform stands as proof that access, equity, and excellence can coexist, and can be built from the ground up. This is more than a magazine. It is a global classroom, a creative lab, and a call to action. For the underrepresented. For the curious. For the future. For youth, by youth — and for the future we intend to build.
